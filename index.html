A glance at the stages of your dog's life.

Puppy Stage

This stage lasts about 1 year, however it can be up to 2 years for some to reach their full size and become more emotionally stable and mentally mature. This is the time of maximum fun, playing, zoomies, naps and toys galore!

Learn More

Adult Stage

This stage lasts from 1-2 years of age up until about 7 years. This is the time where your pooch's behavior will begin to settle as their individual personality come forth. This is the time where bonding and loyalty strengthens over time.

Learn More

Senior Stage

This stage generally begins at about 8 years of age. Most dogs at this age begin to slow down and rest more. As our pets get older this is the time they rely on us to be there for them all the more, as they were for us throughout the years.
